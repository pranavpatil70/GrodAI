What is GrodAI?

Grod AI offers a suite of tools including text-to-speech AI for converting written text into natural-sounding speech, speech-to-files and resources extractor AI for extracting insights from audio content, a simple video editing tool for intuitive video production, and text-to-image AI for generating images from text, enhancing visual content creation.
